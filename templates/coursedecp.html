Amazon

Interviewing at Amazon is a unique experience as the company places great emphasis on its 16 Leadership Principles, such as "Customer Obsession" and "Bias for Action." To succeed, it's essential to understand these principles and be ready to discuss how your experiences align with them. The interview process typically involves a phone screening followed by an onsite interview, often referred to as the "loop," which includes multiple rounds with various team members. Be prepared for a mix of behavioral questions and technical challenges, depending on the role, and focus on demonstrating problem-solving skills, a deep understanding of your domain, and how you embody Amazon's core values.
